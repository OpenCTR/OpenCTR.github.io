Credits

List of people, events, abstract concepts, etc, that have enabled OpenCTR to come into existance.

This is a list of indirect contributors to OpenCTR. For a list of direct contributors, see Authors.

  • Smealum - Indisputably the Grandfather (and mentor) of the Nintendo 3DS Homebrew scene, none of OpenCTR would ever have been possible without his work.
  • Dave Murphy - Developer of devkitARM and libnds - both are predecessors to much of the work in OpenCTR.
  • fincs - For creating the 3DSX format (according to smealum).
  • Binutils - Assembler and Linker used by the OpenCTR toolchain.
  • GCC - Optimizing C/C++ compiler used by the OpenCTR toolchain.
  • Newlib - C Standard Library used in the OpenCTR toolchain.
  • LLVM/Clang - Next generation compiler suite. Intended to become a major part of OpenCTR in the future.
  • Dennis Ritchie - The God among men man who developed the greatest programming language of all time: C.
  • Nintendo - For providing a device worthy of the minds of eager developers and enthusiastic users, and for creating a culture of creativity-suppression that necessitated developers create a way to run homebrew themselves.
  • 3DBrew - For leading the never-ending battle against evil ignorance, by documenting everything related to the Nintendo 3DS, from its hardware and vendor-supplied software, to the service API’s, to recent news regarding the 3DS and its homebrew scene.
